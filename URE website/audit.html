What Your Score Means

45–60 Points: Market Ready

You are a "Plug-and-Play" asset. You likely just need better deal-flow and negotiation strategies. Your positioning is strong, your network knows what you offer, and you understand the mechanics of advisory and board roles. Focus on converting opportunities and optimizing your terms.

30–44 Points: The Operator Trap

You have the experience, but you're still talking like an employee. We need to "productize" your brain. You may have strong operational skills but haven't yet translated them into a marketable advisory offering. Focus on repositioning your expertise for the boardroom rather than the corner office.

Below 30 Points: The Invisible Executive

You are at high risk of the "Retirement Cliff." Your value is high, but your visibility is zero. You have deep expertise that companies need, but you haven't built the positioning, network, or materials to be found and hired. Immediate focus on foundational positioning work is essential.
